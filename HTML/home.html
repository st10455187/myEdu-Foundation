Build A Better Future.

myEdu Foundation is an NPO that assists learners with school-related essentials. We provide them with necessary school supplies such as clothes, shoes, stationery, and healthy meals. At myEdu Foundation, our mission is to support students with financial difficulties.

What We Do

Our mission is to provide vital school supplies to students in need. Together, we can make a significant difference in the lives of many children by ensuring they have the tools necessary for academic success.

How You Can Make a Difference

Your support helps us make a huge impact and provide essential supplies to students in need.
